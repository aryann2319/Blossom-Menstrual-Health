Understand Your Menstrual Health

Discover valuable insights, tips, and guides to help you take control of your menstrual health and make informed decisions about your body.

Menarche

Menarche is when you get your first period. It is an important milestone that marks the beginning of your fertility.

Ovulation

Ovulation is a phase of the female menstrual cycle that involves the release of an egg (ovum) from one of the ovaries.

Pregnancy

Pregnancy is the beautiful phase in which a fetus develops inside a woman's womb. It usually lasts about 40 weeks or 9 months.

Contraception

Birth control, also known as contraception, is the use of medicines, devices, or surgery to prevent pregnancy.

Self Care

Safety

Managing your period effectively is important for maintaining good hygiene, comfort, and overall health.
